Child Safety Standards

Voicle is committed to providing a safe environment for all users. Our platform implements strict age verification and safety measures to protect minors.

Age Verification

Voicle requires all users to be 18 years of age or older. We implement multiple verification methods to ensure compliance:

  • Government-issued ID verification during registration
  • Phone number verification with age-appropriate carriers
  • Continuous monitoring for suspicious account activity
  • Machine learning algorithms to detect potential underage users
