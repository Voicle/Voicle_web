Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 1, 2025

Our Privacy Commitment

At Voicle, privacy isn't just a feature—it's our foundation. As a voice-only, landmark-based dating app, we've built our entire platform around protecting your personal information while creating meaningful connections through authentic voice interactions.

Information We Collect

Voice Data: We temporarily process voice messages for matching purposes only. Voice recordings are encrypted and automatically deleted after 24 hours unless saved by mutual consent.

Location Data: We use approximate landmark-based location (within 5km radius) to suggest nearby matches. We never store your exact GPS coordinates or real-time location.

Profile Information: Basic demographics (age range, interests) provided voluntarily by you. No photos, real names, or social media connections required.

How We Protect Your Data

• End-to-end encryption for all voice communications

• Zero-knowledge architecture - we cannot access your private conversations

• Automatic data deletion policies for temporary content

• No data sharing with third parties for advertising

• Regular security audits and compliance checks

Landmark-Based Privacy

Our unique landmark-based system ensures privacy by design. Instead of tracking your exact location, we use general landmarks and public places to suggest matches within your preferred radius, protecting your home and work locations.

Your Rights

• Complete data deletion upon account termination

• Access to all data we store about you

• Opt-out of data processing at any time

• Control over voice message retention

Government Compliance

Voicle fully complies with all applicable government regulations including GDPR, CCPA, and local data protection laws. We maintain transparency reports and work with regulators to ensure user safety while protecting privacy.

Contact Us

For privacy-related questions or concerns, contact our Data Protection Officer at privacy@voicle.com or through our secure contact form.
