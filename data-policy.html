Data Protection Measures

Minimal Collection: We only collect data that's absolutely necessary for the app to function.

Automatic Deletion: Built-in data lifecycle management ensures old data is automatically removed.

We never sell, rent, or share your personal data with third parties for advertising or marketing purposes. Period.
